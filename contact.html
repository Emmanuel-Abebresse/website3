Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about my services

What types of GIS services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive geospatial services including spatial analysis, cartographic design, land surveying, remote sensing, web mapping development, and spatial database management. Each project is tailored to meet specific client needs.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small mapping projects may take 1–2 weeks, while comprehensive spatial analysis or surveying projects can range from 1–6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! While based in Portsmouth, England, I work with clients globally. Many GIS and cartography projects can be completed remotely with proper data sharing and communication protocols.

What software and tools do you use?

I'm proficient in industry-standard tools including ArcGIS (Pro, Desktop, Online), QGIS, AutoCAD Civil 3D, Python (ArcPy, GeoPandas), PostgreSQL/PostGIS, and various remote sensing platforms like ERDAS Imagine and ENVI.
